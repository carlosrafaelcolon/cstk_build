U.S. in Pakistan

The United States has been launching drone strikes in Pakistan since at least 2004, killing over 3000 people.
U.S. in Yemen

The first drone strike outside of Afghanistan launched by the United States was in Yemen in 2002. After this initial attack, there were no attacks for seven years. In recent years, however, the United States has launched targeted killings by both drones and manned aircraft.
U.S. in Somalia

The United States has been launched attacks against Somalians linked to al-Shabaab and al-Qaeda and East Africa in Somalia for over five years.
Israeli Operations

Israel is believed to have launched drone strikes outside of its borders, including in the Gaza Strip, Egypt, and Syria.
Turkish Operations

Turkey has been launching attacks across the northern border of Iraq for over a decade.
